This dashboard uses geospatial data from Eurostat to display country boundaries on interactive maps. The information below describes the data source, modifications made, and important legal notices.

Geospatial Data Source

The GeoJSON dataset used for country boundaries is sourced from Eurostat (GISCO). This content is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0) licence.

  • Source: Eurostat GISCO Portal
  • Dataset Version: CNTR_RG_60M_2024_4326
  • Access Date: May 7, 2025

Modifications Applied

  • Filtering: Only European countries have been retained.
  • Aggregation: Norway and Svalbard have been merged into a single geographical entity.
  • Standardization: Unique IDs have been added to ensure compatibility with the survey dataset.
  • Visual improvement: Malta shape has been replaced by a bigger version (custom-made) to ensure that the country is clearly visible and some country shape artifacts were fixed.

Statistical Content

The data displayed on this map originates from a JAMRAI Survey. Eurostat is not the author of these results and is not responsible for the data processing or findings shown in this dashboard.
